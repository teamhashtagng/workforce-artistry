Artisans safety is of paramount importance to us

We help artisans to feel secure and confident while providing their services, ultimately enhancing their overall experience.

Client Verification

To protect artisans from potential risks and fraudulent clients we implement a verification process for clients using the platform to ensure that they are genuine and trustworthy.

Transparent Feedback System

We allow artisans to make informed decisions when accepting jobs and to identify and avoid problematic clients, we established a transparent feedback system where artisans can review and rate clients based on their behavior, payment reliability, and overall experience

Safety Information Exchange

We facilitate the exchange of essential safety information between artisans and clients. This can include sharing details about the nature of the project, potential hazards, and any safety equipment or precautions required.

Transparent Communication

We encourage open dialogue to ensure that both artisans and clients are on the same page regarding safety requirements and expectations.

Safety Training and Resources

Mavoye Artistry Offers resources and training materials to artisans to enhance their safety knowledge and skills. Also there are guidelines on safe work practices, proper tool handling, and emergency response protocols.

Insurance Coverage

There is provision of insurance coverage for artisans to protect artisans in case of accidents or injuries that may occur while on the job.

Compliance with Regulations

We stay up to date with relevant safety regulations and ensure that artisans are aware of and comply with them. Also we educate artisans about local safety standards and encourage adherence to ensure their well-being.

Emergency Support

There are clear instructions Provided on how to report incidents and we ensure that immediate assistance is available when needed.
