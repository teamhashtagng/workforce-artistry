We prioritize your safety and peace of mind

We create a secure and trustworthy platform for our clients, enabling them to confidently engage with skilled artisans and receive high-quality services.

Verified Artisans

We only onboard artisans who have undergone a thorough identity verification process, including background checks. This helps establish trust and confidence in the artisans' credibility and reliability.

Service Provider Profiles

Provide detailed profiles for each artisan, including their qualifications, experience, and any relevant certifications. This allows clients to make informed decisions and choose artisans based on their specific needs and preferences.

Ratings and Reviews

We have a rating and review system where clients can provide feedback on their experience with the artisans. This allows other clients to gauge the quality of service and reliability of the artisans before making a selection.

Safety Guidelines and Standards

We clearly communicate safety guidelines and standards that artisans are expected to adhere to during their services. This include proper handling of tools, adherence to safety protocols, and maintaining a clean and safe work environment.

Transparent Communication

There is a transparent communication between clients and artisans through the app. This include a messaging system where clients can discuss project details, ask questions, and address any safety concerns they may have before or during the service.

Insurance Coverage

We provide insurance coverage for clients in case of any accidental damage or injury that may occur during the service. This helps provide an extra layer of protection and peace of mind for clients.

Secure Payments

Our payment process within the app is secure and encrypted to protect clients' financial information. We use trusted payment gateways and comply with relevant security standards.

Emergency Support

There is a support system in place to handle any emergency situations or safety-related issues that clients may encounter. We have clear instructions on how to report incidents and ensure that they are promptly addressed.
